About Attendance Management System

Our Attendance Management System simplifies the process of tracking and managing attendance. Follow the steps below to learn more:

Step 1: Secure Login

Users can log in using their secure credentials to access the system.

Step 2: Course and Student Selection

Select the course, year, and semester to filter the student list.

Step 3: Mark Attendance

Mark students as Present, Absent, or On-Duty effortlessly.And submit the attendance.

Step 4: View Attendance History

View attendance records for previous days to monitor progress.

Step 5: Download Attendance Records

Export attendance data for offline use or reporting purposes.

Step 6: Manage Student Details

Add or update student information to keep records accurate and up-to-date.
